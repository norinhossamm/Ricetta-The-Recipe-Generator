About Ricetta

                  Ricetta was founded in 2001 by Nora as a home cooking blog to record her favorite family recipes. 
              Today, Ricetta has grown into a trusted resource for home cooks with more than 3,000 tested recipes, guides, 
              including our famous recipe generator feature drawing over 15 million readers each month from around the world.
Ricetta is here to help you cook delicious meals with less stress and more joy.
